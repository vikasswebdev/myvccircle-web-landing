Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about MyVCCircle's personal finance management tools.

Does MyVCCircle handle my money?

No, MyVCCircle never handles, stores, or transfers your money. We're a tracking and coordination tool that helps you manage your finances while you maintain full control of your funds.

How do Savings Circles work?

You create or join a private group with trusted friends or family. Track monthly contributions, coordinate bids, and manage group finances ‚Äî all payments happen directly between members.

What are VC Points?

VC Points are rewards you earn for consistent app usage, completing financial goals, and learning new money skills. Redeem them for exclusive benefits and features.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we use advanced security protocols to protect your data. We collect only the minimum necessary information to provide smart finance tools, and you control your data at all times.

How does the AI Finance Coach work?

Our AI analyzes your spending patterns and savings habits to provide personalized tips, budget recommendations, and weekly financial summaries to help you build better money habits.

Can I track expenses and rent payments?

Yes! Our Expense Tracker categorizes your spending, and the Rent Pay Assistant helps you track and split rent payments with roommates. Earn VC Points for consistent logging.

Legal Disclaimer

MyVCCircle is a personal finance tracking and coordination tool. We do not hold, transfer, or manage user funds. All payments and financial exchanges occur independently between users. We are not a bank, wallet, NBFC, or licensed chit fund operator.
