Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about MyVCCircle's group savings and lending platform.

How does the bidding system work?

Members bid on the monthly pooled fund by offering interest rates. The highest bidder wins the fund, while others continue saving for future cycles. Once you win, you cannot bid again in the same group.

Is my money safe with MyVCCircle?

Yes, we use advanced security protocols and automated KYC verification to ensure all transactions are safe and transparent. Your funds are protected by robust security measures.

How do I join a MyVCCircle group?

You can either create your own group or join an existing one. Our platform matches you with verified members and helps set up the group parameters.

What are the benefits of group savings?

Group savings offer better interest rates than traditional banks, build community trust, and provide a structured way to save while potentially accessing funds when needed.

How do referral bonuses work?

Invite friends to join MyVCCircle and earn bonuses when they participate in group savings. The more active members you refer, the more rewards you earn.

Can I customize my savings group?

Yes, you can set your preferred group size, contribution amount, and duration. Our platform helps you find or create groups that match your financial goals.
